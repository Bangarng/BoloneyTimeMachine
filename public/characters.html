Line Up



Hero: What do you call the person who graduates last in super school? Hero.

Sidekick: The best in the business, but no desire to lead. You could say his only weakness is how easily he’s persuaded by Hero.


Shadowtaken: H.A.V.V.O.C. member and 100% Class A professional. If she’s not busy memorizing the superhero manual, then she’s rereading the pamphlet, or practicing martial arts, or just kicking everyone's ass.

Mothman: Mothman isn’t actually a member of H.A.V.V.O.C., but the guy who showed up to the acceptance ceremony and got in line. He doesn’t ask to lead, or to be put in the game, he just likes to wear the gear, and that’s ok, because he brings Gatorade to practice and sits in the corner.
